This is the experimental snap, snapd, and snapcraft documentation that is being worked on to eventually become the main source of information about how to use and publish snaps, and how to develop the tools around them.

Most of this documentation can be collaboratively discussed and changed in the respective topic in the forum. See the [documentation guidelines](/t/documentation-guidelines/3798) if you'd like to contribute.
